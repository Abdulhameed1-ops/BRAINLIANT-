1. Who We Are

Brainliant ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered learning platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our Service at brainliant.app.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: When you register, we collect your name, username, email address, and a securely hashed password. We never store your password in plaintext.

Learning data: We store your XP, quiz scores, topic mastery, study streaks, and leaderboard position to power your personalised experience.

Uploaded files: Files you upload are processed to extract text for quiz generation. We do not permanently store your uploaded files. Extracted text is used only during your session.

Usage data: We may collect basic usage information (pages visited, features used) to improve the Service. This data is aggregated and not tied to individual users.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Service
  • To personalise your learning experience (adaptive quizzes, spaced repetition)
  • To display your progress on the leaderboard (only your name, username, and XP are public)
  • To send important account notifications (e.g. security alerts)
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive behaviour

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

5. Data Security

We take security seriously:
